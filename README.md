## 👨‍💻 Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science graduate working at the intersection of **product strategy, design, and technology**.

Driven by curiosity — understanding systems, people, and problems deeply before shaping solutions that are both **intuitive and scalable**.

- 🧠 Research-oriented approach to problem solving
- 🎨 Creative thinking backed by structured execution
- 🤝 Strong collaboration, communication & team alignment
- 🧩 Focus on clarity, usability, and long-term product direction
